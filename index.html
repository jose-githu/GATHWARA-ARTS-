The Studio

Art Rooted in
Place & Purpose

Gathwara Arts is a fine art studio dedicated to safeguarding and advancing Kenyan visual history through cultural authenticity. Based in the heart of the community, every piece emerges from deep reverence for our heritage—capturing the timeless traditions of the Maasai, the complex rhythms of Kamba craftsmanship, and the rich narratives of the Kikuyu people.

We breathe life into ancestry, preserving stories on canvas that text alone cannot hold. If you are curating a cultural showcase, managing a gallery space, or planning an upcoming exhibition and require authentic visual assets to complete your artwork display, connect with us to collaborate on bringing these pieces to your space.
