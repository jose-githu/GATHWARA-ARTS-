Exhibition Displays

Authentic Assets
Curated
For Your Showcase

An exhibition display is a deep storytelling partnership. We collaborate to understand the narrative of your event, your space, and your target audience—then we provide striking visual assets that capture local Kenyan heritage with absolute authenticity.

Cultural showcases, community events, lodge installations, and educational storytelling displays. Every featured collection honors the detailed histories of the Maasai, Kamba, and Kikuyu peoples, crafted to complete your environment.

  • One-on-one layout consultation (on-site or virtual)
  • Carefully coordinated asset selection to fit your timeline
  • Detailed cultural narratives and background profiles for each piece
  • Secure logistics, transport, and delivery across Kajiado and Kenya
  • Community hubs, tourism lodges, and institutional showcases welcome

Community Mentorship

Learn the Craft
With the Studio.
Preserve the Stories

Created for local artists and creators ready to build their skills to a professional standard. Our mentorship programme combines hands-on technical guidance with the cultural deep dives needed to authentically express and preserve Kenyan traditions.

Deepening local ties across Kajiado. Space limited to six mentees per cohort to ensure dedicated 1-on-1 guidance.

  • 12-week intensive programme (in-studio)
  • Portfolio development and local showcase critique sessions
  • All creative materials included in the programme fee
  • Exhibition display opportunity at the programme close
  • Ongoing community network and studio collaboration support

Programme fee: KES 120,000
